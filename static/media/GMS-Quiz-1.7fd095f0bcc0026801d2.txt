## **Section 1: Multiple Choice (2 points each)**
Circle the correct answer.

1. A beam of light passes from water (n = 1.33) into diamond (n = 2.42). What happens to the speed of light?
   - A) Increases
   - B) Decreases
   - C) Stays the same
   - D) Cannot be determined

2. Which of the following scenarios would result in **total internal reflection**?
   - A) Light traveling from air to glass at Brewster‚Äôs angle
   - B) Light traveling from water to diamond at an angle greater than the critical angle

3. A concave mirror forms a **real, inverted, and reduced** image. Which of the following must be true about the object placement?
   - A) The object is between the focal point and the mirror.
   - B) The object is beyond twice the focal length.
   - C) The object is exactly at the focal point.
   - D) The object is between the focal point and twice the focal length.

4. Which of the following correctly describes **spherical aberration** in mirrors?
   - A) It occurs because parallel light rays do not converge at a single focal point.
   - B) It occurs due to the separation of different colors of light.
   - C) It occurs when light waves interfere and cancel out.
   - D) It is caused by multiple reflections inside an optical fiber.

5. A person with **myopia** can see objects clearly at short distances but not at long distances. Which type of lens is used to correct their vision?

   - C) Cylindrical lens
   - D) Bifocal lens
